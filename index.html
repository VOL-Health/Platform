How the System Works

The cards above establish the spine. The explainer below adds the deeper layer: what each stage is revealing, why it matters, and where each view takes the operator next.

VI — Detect
Surface extra volatility above the endemic baseline a building already carries.
›
VI gives leadership a way to see instability before it just feels like daily chaos.
  • Measures: resident demand volatility, acuity movement, census pressure, operational strain, and leadership drag.
  • Reveals: whether instability is building inside the community faster than it is being absorbed.
  • Use case: early detection, portfolio comparison, and conversation reframing away from reactive staffing language.
Open VI
WSI — Measure
Test whether the workforce system can absorb volatility without burning out the operation.
›
WSI clarifies whether current stability is healthy, fragile, or being held together by stretch and cost.
  • Measures: coverage resilience, schedule instability, financial exposure, pipeline weakness, and workforce drag.
  • Reveals: the difference between real elasticity and burnout-stabilized operations.
  • Use case: budget conversations, market development logic, and workforce strategy alignment.
Open WSI
SHAI — Align
Translate diagnostic insight into the right next operational response.
›
SHAI is the action layer. It helps operators move from “what is happening?” to “what should we do next?”
  • Operator layer: stabilizing moves inside the building or portfolio.
  • Provider layer: workforce solutions that reduce volatility now while strengthening future stability.
  • Use case: recommendations, next-step framing, and aligned intervention logic.
Open SHAI
Map — Visualize
Make the current state and future trajectory visible in one operating frame.
›
The map is where the story becomes intuitive. It lets leaders see what their current state looks like and imagine what a better future state could be.
  • Shows: current position based on the latest VI and WSI scores.
  • Clarifies: which zone the community is operating in and why.
  • Use case: executive storytelling, portfolio review, and movement toward managed or optimized stability.
